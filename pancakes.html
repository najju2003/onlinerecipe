Pancakes Recipe

Back to Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients together.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and add milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup and enjoy!
